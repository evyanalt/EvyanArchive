Project Archive

All of my tests, experiments, and visual demos. Some of which turned into full stories.
There are lots of flashing lights and colors in these projects, so proceed with caution.
Also some of these are impact-heavy, so be careful, because your browser or pc might crash.
Some of these projects don't work on all browsers so you might have to switch to chrome, because almost all of these seem to work on there.

Top-picks

  • PC Crasher - Version 7
    Complete chaos in the console and on the page itself.
  • Dream Engine
    A whole new world of interactive visuals.
  • The finale
    The final part of the ASCII evolution story. It is recommended to play the other parts first.
  • The Effects (Visuals)
    A whole bunch of visual effects that can be controlled by a separate tab/window.
